Enroll With a Hospital:
Enrolling with a hospital gives you the feature of submitting your symptoms, problems and health-related issues to get a detailed report and recommendations from a doctor of the applied hospital. Enrolling with a hospital also gives your health access to our artifical intelligence models well capable of diagnosing and counter-thinking your diseases. Note:- Hospitals may or may not ask for payment for the regularly scheduled checkup However, the first virtual check-up is complementary from our side
